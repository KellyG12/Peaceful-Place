Welcome to The Peaceful Place, a Booknerd's Dream, as one might say!

We are a private library based in Midrand, with an inhouse Cafe.

The purpose of our site is to ensure curious minds may enquire about their interests before making the journey to us.

Every week we showcase the top 4 books in some of the genres we stock. Should you be looking for something in particular, send us a little love letter on our contact page and we will get back to you ASAP!

Please feel free to visit us and enjoy our cozy-corner reading nook if you just need a wee adventure down Fantasy Lane!
We are so pleased that you stumbled upon our site!

Please take note that we follow COVID regulations, screening will take place upon entry, and a mask is a necessity.
